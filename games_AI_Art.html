People felt as if they were indeed speaking with a therapist, not a bot. Today, the experiments in artificial intelligence, natural language processing, machine learning have been fully commercialized to track both the virtual and physical world, largely for surveillance or commodity. Of course, in implementing it, one is also enhancing Google's algorithms and their understanding and ultimately control of the internet.
